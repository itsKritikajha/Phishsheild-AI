## Slide 2: The Problem (Real-World Impact)
- **Bullet Points:**
  - Over 3.4 billion phishing emails are sent globally every single day.
  - India is among the top 3 global targets for cyber fraud.
  - Hackers are using AI to generate flawless website clones and typosquatting domains.
  - Traditional signature-based antivirus cannot catch Zero-Day phishing links in time.
- **Visual:** A large, bold statistic (e.g., "3.4 Billion Daily Attacks" in red).

## Slide 3: The Solution (Phishield AI)
- **Concept:** A multi-modal, enterprise-grade AI ecosystem that predicts threats *heuristically* rather than relying on outdated blacklists.
- **Core Pillars:**
  1. **Real-time ML Prediction** (Random Forest heuristics)
  2. **Live Browser Protection** (Chrome Extension)
  3. **Enterprise UI/Analytics** (Dashboard with PDF Reporting)
- **Visual:** 3 clean icons representing the three pillars.

## Slide 4: Key Features (The "Wow" Factor)
- **Bullet Points:**
  - **Live Threat Intel Map:** Global attack simulation.
  - **Explainable AI:** Generates human-readable reasoning for *why* a site is blocked.
  - **Multi-Modal Scanning:** URLs, NLP Email scanning, and Typosquatting clone detection.
- **Visual:** Screenshots of the UI (specifically the AI Threat Gauge and Reputation Score).

## Slide 5: The Browser Extension (The Game Changer)
- **Concept:** The biggest differentiator of our project. It brings the AI directly to the user's browsing session.
- **How it works:** Instant popup warning with Threat Level, Confidence Score, and Reputation.
- **Visual:** A large screenshot of the Chrome extension popup analyzing a dangerous site with a red warning.

## Slide 6: Machine Learning Pipeline
- **Dataset:** 22,400 synthesized URLs mimicking global phishing trends.
- **Model:** Compared Logistic Regression vs Random Forest. Optimized Random Forest achieved **99%+ Accuracy**.
- **Features Extracted:** Lexical markers (HTTPS, length, special chars, IP obfuscation, URL shorteners).
- **Visual:** The `feature_importance.png` or `confusion_matrix.png` generated by our training script.
